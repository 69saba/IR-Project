Through information retrieval, we find material of an unstructured nature that satisfies an information need from within large collections of recipe databases. We create a website that using a large dataset of recipes from all over the world to intelligently generate new and improved recipes having not only great taste but also contain the required nutritional value. And also, these recipes due to their unique taste and nutritious values attract a wide range of individuals as well as restaurants all around the world. The dataset we use roughly 1.5 million recipes and the objective is achieved by using various data scraping tools.
